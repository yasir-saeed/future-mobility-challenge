About

My Name is Yasir Saeed, and I'm a Computer Science major at the University of Windsor, and this is my submission for the OBD-II Interpretation and Visualization Challenge.

This web application, built with Python, HTML and CSS and the web framework Flask. It loads a dataset from a car trip, and graphs each column in the dataset with respect to time. It also gives the corresponding PID (Parameter ID) Code and Description of the column.

I have also added an interactive map, which graphs the car's journey if the latitude and longitude of the vehicle is logged throughout the trip.

In conclusion, I am proud of the work I have completed through my knowledge in Computer Science and programming, and overall it has been a valuable learning experience for me.
